Source dataset: Natural Earth 1:110m Admin 0 country polygons.

Source project: Natural Earth, https://www.naturalearthdata.com/

License: Natural Earth public domain.

Projection and simplification: coordinates are stored as simplified WGS84
longitude/latitude polygon rings for this terminal prototype. Rendering uses a
simple equirectangular projection to a terminal cell grid.

Grouping: North America and South America are grouped together as
`The Americas`. Antarctica is included as its own continent. Transcontinental
and ambiguous source records are assigned to the broad visual continent that
best preserves recognizable terminal-scale shapes; country borders are not
represented by this prototype.
